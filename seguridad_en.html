Safety

THE THERMONOX® TREATMENT OFFERS OUR CUSTOMERS AND USERS THE FOLLOWING GUARANTEES
  • Use in production plants with danger of dust explosion (EU Regulation 94/9/EG, also known as ATEX 100a)
  • Meets the requirements of classification ex II 3 D
  • It is controlled and authorized for permanent use in zone 22
PREVENTS HEAT DAMAGE
In each ThermoNox® heater there are 3 thermostats that ensure that the temperature for disinsection remains constant between 50º C and 60º C thanks to an automatic on and off system.

No heat damage
SAFETY AND EFFECTIVENESS
The system has been approved by the FSA (Research Institute for the Safety of Applied Systems and Occupational Medicine) for use in the extermination of pests in the cultivation and handling of cereals and flour through heating to about 50°C.
PROTECTION BEFORE THE INSURANCE COMPANY
A leading German insurance company has found that the system does not increase risks and therefore is not a factor in increasing the insurance policy.
