ThermoNoxilo - Silo treatment

Pest control + improved hygiene in silo cells


In silo treatment, one or more ThermoNoxilo heaters are installed in, for example, a flour silo to control pests or to improve hygiene (mold, caking, etc.). The air inside the silo circulates constantly and heats up until it reaches a lethal temperature of approximately 55º C. The duration of a treatment, including the phase of heating and temperature maintenance, is approximately 48 hours.

Silo heater: ThermoNoxilo SO 18

The ThermonoXilo silo heater is introduced into the silo cell through the manhole by using a specially designed winch.


Technical data:


  • Robust aluminum cylinder for placement inside the silo
  • 18 kW heat output + powerful axial fan
  • External control cabinet to be placed outside the silo
  • External control box to place outside the silo.

Optional: silo winch

With the silo winch, two units can be placed independently inside the cell of the silo. All components can be disassembled into manageable sizes and stored on a pallet together with two pieces of ThermoNoxil.

Safety:

The appearance of dangerous and explosive atmospheres in a flour silo or even grain. These closed containers are classified according to the regulations for protection against explosions such as ATEX Zone 21. ThermoNoxilo and the winch for silos are designed to be safe for use under the conditions provided for it and approved by DEKRA. In addition to certification ATEX (type examination), the ThermoNoxilo also has the IECEx certification, common at the international.
