Applications

In the course of the last 15 years of ThermoNox® it has been shown that for our system there are countless applications. Traditionally used in compartments individual buildings, entire buildings or specific areas of a building, but also can be used in disinfection chambers.
Sensitive devices such as electronic components, laboratory equipment, cigarette machines (Hauni) or the packaging area are no problem for ThermoNox®.
The Swiss machinery and equipment manufacturer Bühler has been certifying that its products can be treated by ThermoNox® for years.
THE SECTORS IN WHICH THERMONOX® IS MOST COMMONLY USED INCLUDE:

Alimentación

Food factories, mills, bakeries, pasta production, baby nutrition, gastronomy etc.

Food for animals

Pet Food plants, concentrated feed factories.

Non-food

Tobacco, textile, wood pests and disinfection industries according to the IPPC standard (ISPM 15).

DRYING BUILDINGS AND ENVIRONMENTAL IMPROVEMENT
For a few years now, bed bug control has become a sector in which ThermoNox® has proven to be particularly effective:
  • Private homes, residences, official institutions
  • Hospitality: hotels, motels, youth hostels
  • Means of transport: train, plane and vehicles
LIMITATIONS OF THE USE OF THERMONOX®
  • Disinsection of products (large amounts of products such as grain or flour have an insulating effect)
  • Outside areas of the factory, permanently open areas
  • Highly leaky buildings (open roof loose wood partition)
